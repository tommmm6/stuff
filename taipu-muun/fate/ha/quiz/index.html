This quiz appears when visiting the ruins in the Einzbern Forest (Outskirts) on Day 4 (October 11).

The reader gets a choice of either the "Elementary", "Moderate", or "Show me your best" difficulties.

An extra quiz also appears if the reader chooses to stay at the castle, and visits the ruins at night-time.
